The Science Behind Memory

Have you ever wondered how your brain stores your favorite song or a friend's name?

Types of Memory

  • Short-Term Memory: Holds information for a short time.
  • Long-Term Memory: Stores information for a long time.
  • Working Memory: Helps you think and solve problems.

Fun Facts About Memory

Sleep helps your memory.

Smells can bring back memories.

Writing helps you remember better.

Tips for a Better Memory

  • Use rhymes or tricks to remember.
  • Review things again and again.
  • Teach others what you learn.

Memory helps you keep important things in your mind.
